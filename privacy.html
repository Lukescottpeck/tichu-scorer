Privacy Policy — Tichu Scorer

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Tichu Scorer is a simple scorekeeping app for the Tichu card game. Your privacy is straightforward because the app does not collect anything.

Data collection

Tichu Scorer does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.

Data storage

All information you enter — team names, scores, round history, and saved games — is stored locally on your device only, using your browser's local storage. None of it ever leaves your device or reaches any server. The developer cannot see it.

Deleting your data

You can remove your data at any time by using the in-app reset and clear buttons, or by uninstalling the app / clearing the app's storage in your device settings.

Children

The app contains no objectionable content and collects no data, but it is a general-audience utility and is not directed at children under 13.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the developer at the email listed on the app's Google Play store page.
